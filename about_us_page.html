WHAT WE DO

With the help of our finest refined data management algorithm, we help you sort, arrange, compare and extract information from different file formats. We give you the Super power to compare two totally different documents in different file formats. With Docufix you have the potential to know exactly the difference and similarities between two files, extract texts from the files and also download the files in file formats like: .docx, .pdf

HOW IT WORKS

This tool is as easy as picking up your mobile phone to send a text message. Simply goto the comparison page, paste your copied texts to compare, or click on file upload to upload your file type and extract or compare.
